    Job Description

  • Ensuring that website design, layout, and coding is optimized for search.
  • Working with the web designer to ensure consistent standards of SEO.
  • Performing regular tests on website SEO rankings.
  • Troubleshooting technical SEO issues for the website.
  • Giving feedback to the Digital Marketing team about SEO/SEM opportunities.
  • Researching the latest SEO guidelines and implementing them.
  • Assisting with integrations to enhance or maintain SEO competitiveness.
  • Planning website SEO contingencies for problems that may arise.
  • Updating MarComm Managers and team members on SEO standards and changes.
  • Execute on-page SEO strategies across a diverse mix of web properties.
  • Conduct onsite and offsite technical audits; identify issues and opportunities; implement fixes and updates using SEO best practices.
  • Integrate external services with Drupal, often via APIs, RESTful APIs and formats (JSON, XML).
  • Conduct QA/testing and implement scope changes as needed.
  • Monitor Core Web Vitals - Largest Contentful Paint (LCP), First Input Delay (FID), and Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS) of all the properties.
  • Work closely with the MarComm team to source and develop new and unique web content that will enhance the user experience and drive conversions via organic traffic growth.

    Requirements

  • A bachelors degree in computer science, programming, or web development.
  • A minimum of 2 years front-end development and web design experience.
  • Certification in SEO or Google Analytics will be beneficial.
  • Working knowledge of standards-compliant, responsive, PHP, HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript
  • Advanced knowledge of Core Web Vitals (LCP, FID CLS) and Drupal.
  • Experience working with debugging tools such as Chrome Inspector and Firebug.
  • Ability to work with back-end SEO elements such as regex rewrites rules, robots.txt, metadata, and related skills.
  • Experienced using SEO tools such as SEMRush, and Search Console.
  • Ability to work independently with minimal oversight.
  • Organized, deadline-driven, and detail-focused.
  • Proficiency in front-end programming languages, such as HTML, CSS, or JS.
  • Experience with back-end programming languages is beneficial.

About Company

Liz Elting and Phil Shawe met at New York University Stern School of Business in 1991. Having spent several years working in the translation industry, Ms. Elting felt there was a need for a service-minded enterprise that could deliver complex projects quickly and accurately. In 1992, Elting and Shawe founded TransPerfect to fill that gap. Their mission: to provide the highest quality language services to leading businesses worldwide. With no external financing, TransPerfect grew from its beginnings as a two-person company operating out of a dorm room into one of the worlds most reputable language and business services companies.
